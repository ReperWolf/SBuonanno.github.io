Guardian of Salem

COM 419 Video Game Design Studio

Overview

Within the class students had to create teams of 3 and each group member must have a role of programmer, designer or artist; There was also an additional role of producer so 1 person would be responsible for 2 roles. The project duration was 1 full semester or roughly 4 months with the expectation of building a game as close to completion as possible with a trailer video. For this project my team and I created a demo along with a trailer video.

Game Concept

The goal concept is a puzzle-based playable novel taking place in Salem during the Salem witch trials. Our main character is Ani(based on the latin word "anima" meaning soul) who is 16 and finds herself in a situation of the mayor accusing her of witchcraft. This unfortunately leads to her death and the player begins their journey as Ani who has no recollection of her past and must uncover the truth. The player will face different types of challenges and discover the dark secrets that the mayor tries so desperately to hide while pursuing a parallel journey of finding out who Ani is. Overall, the theme is supposed to give an eerie atmosphere, provide a self discovery journey and face the harsh reality that lies within the game.

Role: Programmer and Producer

Within the producer role I was in charge of keeping our group coordindated outside the classroom and made sure we were on track with our project's scope. I would touch base with my team members if they were having issues and assisted them in troubleshooting whatever problem they were having difficulty with. My goal as the producer was to ensure our scope was resonable for the timeline, have communication within the group and evenly divide the work if applicable.

As the programmer I implemented and integrated all art assets and level designs that my team members created. I also informed them on what would be achieveable within the time frame that we were given from a programmer's perspective. To add extra challenge within our game I proposed that we create a forest with a hidden path and if the player looked carefully enough there were slightly different colored trees to guide them to the next area[time stamp - 2:09 of trailer video].

Software Used

Godot as a game engine is known to have a quick learning curve as the programming language is based off of Python. Its UI is easy to naviagte and utilize the tools available within the program. At the time I was most familiar with Godot and it allowed us to implement what we needed for the game.
